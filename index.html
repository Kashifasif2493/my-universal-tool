About TextMaster Pro

TextMaster Pro is a high-performance, privacy-focused toolkit designed by Kashif Asif. Our mission is to provide essential digital utilities—from text manipulation to AI-driven assistance—all in one place.

Unlike other online tools, we process everything locally in your browser. This means your data never touches our servers, making it 100% secure and incredibly fast.

Whether you're a developer, writer, or student, TextMaster Pro is built to simplify your workflow with a clean, ad-free interface.

Privacy Policy

1. No Data Storage: We do not collect, store, or share any text, passwords, or files you process using our tools.

2. Local Execution: All logic (including Password Generation and Binary Conversion) happens on your device using JavaScript.

3. Cookies: We use minimal browser storage only to remember your theme preferences and UI state.

Your privacy is our priority. You can use TextMaster Pro offline once the page is loaded.
